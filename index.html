MusicScape

Welcome to MusicScape ! Here at MusicScape, we utilize the Soundcloud API to help users search & stream music from SoundCloud.com

Instructions to get started:

  1. In the search bar above, type the name of the artist you want to hear.
  2. Select the desired artist from the results provided to pull up their available tracks.
  3. Select the track you want to play to begin streaming it.
  4. Escape into Music!

Keep In Mind:

SoundCloud.com is a site primarily dedicated to providing exposure to lesser known artists. Because of this, you will not be able to stream many of the more popular artists. So, get creative.
